How We Work

OUR WORK PROCESS

Discovery Phase

01. Consultation & Design Discovery

Our specialists meet you at your property. We perform full measurements, discuss materials (hardwood, vinyl, paint colors, vanity styles), and explore your aesthetic options.

Planning Phase

02. Transparent Scope & Estimates

We send you a detailed line-item breakdown of your project. There are no hidden fees or surprise charges. We list materials, labor, and timeline parameters.

Execution Phase

03. Execution by Licensed Teams

Our experienced crews execute the design. We handle demo, preparation, installation, and clean-up. We respect your property and keep clean margins.

Handover Phase

04. The Handover Audit

We inspect the results side-by-side with you. We check thresholds, caulking lines, hardware, and finishes, ensuring the final output meets our high standard of craft.
